# HR People Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

##  Project Overview
This project showcases an **HR People Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze employee data and generate actionable HR insights.  
The dashboard helps organizations understand workforce structure, attrition drivers, and performance & promotion trends to support **data-driven HR decision-making**.

---

##  Project Objectives
- Analyze workforce demographics and employee distribution  
- Identify key drivers of employee attrition  
- Evaluate employee performance and promotion trends  
- Build interactive KPIs and visuals for HR stakeholders  

---

##  Dashboard Pages

### 1️ Employee Insight
- Total & active employees
- Workforce distribution by age group, education, and department
- Overtime participation analysis
- Top 10 employees by experience, salary, and salary hike

### 2️ Attrition Insight
- Total attrition & attrition rate
- Attrition analysis by age group and experience level
- Attrition by job satisfaction, overtime, and income band
- Attrition drivers using decomposition tree
- Gender-wise attrition analysis

### 3️ Performance & Engagement
- Total promoted employees & promotion rate
- Promotion rate by job role and gender
- High performer analysis
- Top 10 high performers with income and age

---

##  Key KPIs
- Total Employees  
- Active Employees  
- Attrition Rate  
- Promotion Rate  
- Average Income  
- Average Attrition Age  
- High Performers Count
